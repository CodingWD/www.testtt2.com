Future Robot Tech meets you on 2019 Intel China Industrial IoT Ecology Open Day
2019-06-13

On June 12, 2019, Future Robot Technology Co., Limited was invited to participate in the "2019 Intel China Industrial IoT Ecology Open Day" seminar held by Intel in Wuxi, Jiangsu.

 

 

At this seminar, Future Robot Technology Co., Ltd vision controller products were shown to the audience and got great impression. In the speech part of the conference, Mr. Yang Chunbao, the general manager of Future Robot Technology Co., Ltd, introduced the new products.

 

 

Let me introduce these products with some parameters:

 

1. S100 Industrial Smart Camera

S100 series Industrial Smart Camera is based on Intel X86 CPU+FPGA platform, equipped with global shutter CMOS sensor, which is ideally suitable for high-speed moving object inspection, like Vision Positioning, Vision Inspection, Vision Guidance, Intelligent Sorting, Barcode Recognition and Defect Inspection, etc.

 

2. V300 Vision Controller

V300 series is high-integrated, high-performance, ultra-compact professional Vision Controller, designed for Barcode Recognition, Defect Inspection, Size measurement, Positioning Guidance and wider vision application fileds.

3. E310 Vision Fanless Embedded IPC

E310 series is designed with ultra-compact size, reinforced enclosure, multi interfaces, suitable for Intelligent Transportation, Facility Management, Industrial Automation, Smart Warehousing, Machine Vision, CNC, Internet of Things, etc.


4. C100 4-port PCIe Gige Vision PoE Frame Grabber

C100 series frame grabber can be integrated with fanless computers to deploy in applications with limited space. It’s ideally suitable for Intelligent Transportation, Vision Inspection, Surveillance, AOI, Medical Imaging, Data Center and Industrial Automation, etc.

Let me share the latest Machine Vision product family, for more products please do not hesitate to contact with Future Robot Technology Co., Limited. Thank you.


Finally, we specially thank to support of Intel Corporation.
