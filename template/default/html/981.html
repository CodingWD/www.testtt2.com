Build a Dream, Build the Future
2019-03-16

Dear honored guests,

 

As member of Intel IoT Solutions Alliance, Future Robot Technology Co., LTD will jointly hold the “Build a Dream, Build the Future - Machine Vision and Motion Control Product Release Conference” with Intel Corporation in Shenzhen on March 26, 2019.

 

In the past year, the fields of AI, Big Data, 3D Imaging and Robot Process Automation have achieved unprecedented development. Intelligent manufacturing characterized by the accelerated integration of  information technology and manufacturing industry has become the main trend of global manufacturing industry. In the future, technologies related to Industry 4.0 are driving more changes in the manufacturing industry, which will promote continuous improvement of comprehensive cost control, efficiency and quality of manufacturing.

 

Through this release conference, you will:

1.  Obtain the most authoritative innovative information, industry insights and trends

2.  Deeply learn about machine vision and motion control applications in automation equipment industry

3.  Master the trend of machine vision technology development

4.  Experience Intel's innovative technology, innovative ecological achievements and technological innovation pioneer team's project display


Here, we sincerely invite you to take time out of your busy schedule to come, to share the innovative results of machine vision and motion control products and industry development trend, to discuss the implementation of Artificial Intelligence projects in the industrial field and to create industrial future together.
