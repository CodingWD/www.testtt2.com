Product release-New member of Future Robot Tech Machine Vision product family
2019-06-14

Congrats! Future Robot Technology Co., Limited got new member of Machine Vision product family, V300 series Vision Controller, which is based on Intel Apollo Lake SoC+FPGA platform, designed with professional vision control function and high performance of image processing. It’s a great choice for Smart Factory majority applications.

 

V300 series is high-integrated, high-performance, ultra-compact and zero noise professional Vision Controller, designed for Barcode Recognition, Defect Inspection, Size measurement, Positioning Guidance and wider vision application fileds.

 

Features:

l  Intel Apollo Lake N4200/J4205 Quad Core CPU+FPGA

l  HDMI+DP dual display

l  3LAN (2 Intel i210)

l  Isolated 4DI+4DO

l  2 Light Control

l  1 RS232+ 1 RS485

l  Wide temperature: -20°C~60°C

l  24V DC input

l  Fanless compact design, wall mounting
