ODM/OEM Customization Service of Future Robot
Provide customers with high-qualitycustomized hardware platform solutionsthat best meet application requirements。

ODM/OEM customization services rely on the company's rich experience in hardware R8D and manufacturingfor many years, and provide customers with high-quality, customized hardware platforms that best meetapplication needs by integrating superior technologies and resources within the company and upstream anddownstream Solutions (including but not limited to motherboards and modules based on x86, ARM and other platforms。

  • Rich custom research and development experience

    Future Robot has provided professional ODM/OEM customization services for domestic and foreign first-line equipment/system manufacturers for a long time, and has rich experience in research and development of customized products that meet the complex application requirements of various vertical industries。

  • Quick marketization

    Future Robot allocates sufficient resources for each ODM/OEM customization project to ensure the first response to customer needs, efficient collaboration with customers, shortening the R&D cycle, and helping customers quickly realize product marketization。

  • Cost control

    Future Robot has introduced cost assessment from the beginning of customer formulation of product specifications, and has strict cost control at the R&D end, and shares the cost advantages of procurement channels with customers to help customers reduce costs while maintaining the same quality。
