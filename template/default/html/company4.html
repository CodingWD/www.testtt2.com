About us
About us
  • Our Business

    Future Robot Technology Co., Ltd. is located in Shenzhen, China. It is a National High-tech Enterprise focusing on the fields of intelligent manufacturing and artificial intelligence. The company integrates R&D, production, system integration and sales services. With superb R&D strength as the core and market demand as the guide, it provides embedded products for global enterprises and individual users. The products are mainly used in industrial automation, robots, and machine vision.

  • Team

    The company's hardware technology core team is composed of x86, FPGA, DSP hardware and software engineers, BIOS engineers, layout engineers, algorithm engineers, system development engineers and test engineers. The team members have more than 15 years of industry experience in industrial computers, machine vision who has rich project experience in the field of motion control.
