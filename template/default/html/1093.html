Future Robot Meets you at Intel® Industrial Solution Builders Summit 2019
2019-09-16

On September 16th, 2019, Future Robot Technology Co., Limited was invited to participate in “Intel® Industrial Solution Builders Summit 2019" held in Shanghai. Numerous industry experts gathered to discuss the industry development and latest applications of the Industrial Internet of Things, experience the demonstration of the latest technologies and solutions, and listen to the real sharing and feedback of end users.

At the opening of the summit, Dr. Chen Wei, Vice President of Intel IoTG and General Manager of Intel China-Internet of Things Division, delivered a speech, proposing that Intel will work together with industry partners to focus on exploring the industry overall solutions, building an open and cooperative industrial Internet of Things ecosystem.

Subsequently, Ms. Christine Boles, Vise President of Intel's Industrial and Energy Solutions Division, gave a wonderful speech on the Intel Industrial IoT Outlook.

In the afternoon, in the smart manufacturing sub-forum and digital energy sub-forum, Intel brought industry partners and participants to share real-life case studies of industry applications, and jointly explore how to build an Intel-based Internet of Things solution to subvert traditional manufacturing, Logistics, Industry and power industry.

Amos Yang from Future Robot Technology Co., Limited shared his professional point of view and progress on Industrial Machine Vision solutions and applications.

During the summit, Future Robot’s Vision Server was unveiled, which gained high praise and recognition.

Experts and customers from domestic and abroad met and discussed about our showcase at the summit, such as Vision Controller, Industrial Smart Camera, Fanless Embedded Computer, Motion Controller, Frame Grabbers etc.
