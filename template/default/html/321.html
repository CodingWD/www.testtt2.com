About Smart Manufacturing


Manufacturers today face many challenges. As competition intensifies and customer demands become more and more diversified, enterprises need to continuously optimize production lines in production, improve production efficiency and save production costs, while traditional manufacturing can no longer meet market demand. Therefore, it is imperative to transform and upgrade from traditional manufacturing to intelligent manufacturing. Intelligent Manufacturing (Intelligent Manufacturing, IM) is based on the deep integration of the new generation of information and communication technology and advanced manufacturing technology. It runs through all aspects of manufacturing activities such as design, production, management, and service. , self-adaptive and other functions of the new production method. Smart manufacturing takes the smart factory as the carrier, takes the intelligence of key manufacturing links as the core, and is based on end-to-end data flow and supported by Internet communication.


Through intelligent manufacturing, enterprises can have the following advantages:

1. Monitor the situation of each production link in real time and adjust the production strategy in time;

2. More precise control of machine operation and identification of defects, thereby improving production efficiency;

3. Make predictions through big data and help managers make production plans;

4. It is easier to meet the diversified customization of customers;

5. Reduce production costs.
