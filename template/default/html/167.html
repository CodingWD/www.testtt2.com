Application of E67I 4U Industrial PC in the field of environmental monitoring
2023-09-14

Background Introduction


In the development of modern society, people's quality of life is constantly improving, they are paying more and more attention to environmental protection, and have stricter requirements for the environment. In order to create a healthy living environment and promote the harmonious coexistence between man and nature, environmental testing plays an important role. Environmental monitoring refers to tracking changes in environmental quality and determining the level of environmental quality through the detection of the content and emissions of various substances that have an impact on humans and the environment, providing the basis and guarantee for environmental management, pollution control, and other work. Through environmental monitoring, environmental pollution data can be obtained, providing data support for identifying pollution sources and formulating solutions, which is of great significance to environmental protection. The environmental monitoring system uses an industrial computer as the control center. By installing sensors or other monitoring equipment, it can position and monitor environmental factors such as air, water, and soil, and perform real-time and accurate monitoring of environmental factors such as temperature, humidity, atmospheric pressure, and light intensity. 


Requirements


Taking the sewage treatment online monitoring system as an example, the industrial computer serves as the calculation and control center and can monitor the operating status of the equipment in real-time, including current, voltage, and other parameters; perform real-time data processing and analysis on the data collected by monitoring point sensors or professional instruments, It also outputs various charts and reports to support managers in adjusting strategies in a timely manner. In environmental monitoring systems, industrial computers serve as core equipment and have high application requirements:

1. Powerful performance: As a computing center, industrial computers need to process and analyze multiple aspects of data in real-time at the same time. Powerful performance and data computing capabilities are indispensable conditions for monitoring system computers;

2. Real-time: Monitoring is carried out under real-time conditions, and any abnormalities must be reported to managers in a timely manner;

3. Stable and reliable: Environmental monitoring is carried out 24/7, and industrial computers should meet the requirements of stable operation for a long time.


Product Recommend 


The specifications of the Future Robot U67I 4U rack-mounted industrial computer are as follows:

  • Intel 13th generation Raptor Lake series processor platform

  • Maximum 128GB DDR4 U-DIMM memory

  • Up to 3 displays: VGA/HDMI/DP

  • 2*LAN (1*Intel i219-LM GbE LAN, 1*Intel i225-V GbE LAN)

  • 2*USB3.0, 4*USB 3.2

  • 1*RS232/422/485

  • 1*PS/2 Connector

  • 2*PCIe x16 expansion slot supports dual graphics cards


Product Application


Take the water quality online monitoring system as an example. The water quality online monitoring system consists of a water quality monitoring station and a remote monitoring center. The water quality monitoring station is mainly responsible for water sample collection, water quality analysis, data collation, and remote transmission, including total organic carbon (TOC). , total phosphorus (TP), COD, five conventional parameters (water temperature, pH value, dissolved oxygen DO, conductivity, turbidity), and other online automatic monitors. The collected data is transmitted to the U67I 4U industrial computer through the data acquisition module. Industrial computers are used to realize on-site monitoring and control of environmental data elements of water quality analysis units. The monitored data are recorded, analyzed, and summarized in real-time and the results are output to form daily, monthly, and annual reports to provide environmental management personnel with data and Technical Support.


Product Advantages


1. Powerful computing power and excellent performance: The U67I series 4U industrial computer is based on Intel's 13th generation Raptor Lake platform processor, providing faster P-core (performance core) and more E-core (energy efficiency core), while supporting DDR4/DDR5 and PCIe 4.0/5.0.

2. Rich interfaces and multiple expansions: U67I is equipped with a variety of expansion interfaces, which can be compatible with different software, hardware, and terminal equipment, and handle multiple tasks at the same time.

3. Rugged and anti-interference: U67I adopts a high-strength aluminum alloy outer box, which is suitable for industrial sites, supports long-term trouble-free operation, can work normally at lower and higher temperatures, and has dust-proof, moisture-proof, earthquake-proof, and other functions. As well as strong anti-electromagnetic interference capabilities, the front panel of U67I is equipped with a lock to prevent abnormal operation.
